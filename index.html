🔒 Privacy & Data Notice
Read-only access only. PhishGuard Pro requests the minimum Gmail permission (gmail.readonly) — it can read emails but cannot send, delete, or modify anything. Email content is fetched on demand, analyzed in memory, and never stored. Content is only sent to an AI service when you explicitly click the "AI Analyze" button. Your OAuth tokens are stored in a secure server-side session and are never exposed to the browser.
